antler.io

What is antler.io?

A small reminder app that allows you to connect with the people around you without immediately exchanging details.

How does antler.io work?

  1. Sign up with your email.
  2. Quickly connect with people.
  3. Get two digest emails - that night and a week later.

Why antler.io?

Meetups & conferences are hectic. You run out of business cards, are late for the next session, get over-stimulated and forget all the cool cats you met that day. Antler.io will remind you, via digest email, the day you met somebody and a week after that.
And then? Never again, that's it.

Where is my list of connections?

We'll send this to you in an email (two in fact). Antler is intentionally simple online - there are no likes or followers, just a connect & confirm button.

What about privacy?

If you sign up and connect with a fellow user, we will send them your email address. Everybody else can see your avatar, first name & last initial.

We may send you an email or two about the service.

What else?

Well, if you must know, antler.io is still a wee fawn. There's no location authentication (your buddy in Kuala Lumpur can connect with you) and there are plenty of other issues.
